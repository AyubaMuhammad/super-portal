Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our Academy

What are your admission requirements?

We accept children aged 2-15 years. The admission process includes a campus tour, meeting with our staff, and completing the enrollment forms.

What are your operating hours?

We're open Monday to Friday from 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM for special programs.

Do you provide meals for children?

Yes, we provide nutritious meals and snacks that are prepared daily in our kitchen, following dietary guidelines for young children.

What is your teacher-to-student ratio?

We maintain a low teacher-to-student ratio of 1:8 to ensure each child receives personalized attention and care.

Do you offer extracurricular activities?

Yes, we offer various extracurricular activities including music, art, sports, and language classes as part of our curriculum.

How can I schedule a tour?

You can schedule a tour by calling us at +234 567-8900 or by filling out the contact form on this page.
