Enrollment FAQs

Common questions about our enrollment process

What is the enrollment process?

After submitting your enrollment form, we'll contact you to schedule a tour and meeting with our director. Once accepted, you'll complete registration paperwork and pay the registration fee.

What are the age requirements?

We accept children from 2 to 6 years old. Children must be 2 years old by September 1st of the enrollment year to join our program.

Is financial aid available?

Yes, we offer limited financial assistance based on family income and circumstances. Please contact our office for more information about financial aid options.

What are your safety protocols?

We maintain strict safety measures including secure facilities, staff background checks, emergency preparedness drills, and comprehensive health policies.
